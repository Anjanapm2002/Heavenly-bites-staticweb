Delivery terms

Orders are typically delivered within 30-60 minutes, depending on your location and traffic conditions.
once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via SMS or email to monitor the delivery in real-time.
We currently deliver within a 10 km radius of our store locations; please check if your address falls within this range before ordering.
For your safety, we offer contactless delivery options where our driver will leave your order at your doorstep and notify you upon arrival.
A nominal delivery fee applies based on your location, and free delivery is available for orders above a specified amount.
